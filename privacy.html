Sayso — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

The short version. Sayso is a word-puzzle game published by WeCraft Solutions. We don't ask you to create an account, and we don't collect your personal information. Your game progress is stored on your device. The only data sharing happens through Google's advertising and the app stores' payment services, described below.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Sayso app ("the app", "we", "us"), published by WeCraft Solutions, handles information when you use it. By using Sayso, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect, sell, or store personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or location on our own servers. We do not require you to sign in or create an account to play.

The app itself works with the following information:

  • Game progress — your level, coins, hearts, stars, daily-challenge streak and settings are saved locally on your device. This never leaves your phone unless you choose to enable cloud save (see §4).
  • Purchase status — if you buy something, our payment provider tells the app which items you own (for example, "ads removed"), so the app can unlock them and restore them later.

2. Advertising

Sayso is free to play and is supported by ads served through Google AdMob. To show ads (including optional "watch a video to earn a reward" ads), AdMob and its partners may collect and process certain information from your device, such as your advertising identifier, device type, general (coarse) location derived from your IP address, and app-interaction data. This is used to serve, cap, and measure ads, and — where permitted — to personalise them.

  • You can reset or limit ad tracking at any time in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads; iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising).
  • Where required by law, we ask for your consent before showing personalised ads and give you the option to choose non-personalised ads.
  • Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. See also How Google uses information from apps that use its services.

3. Purchases

In-app purchases (such as removing ads, the Puzzle Pass subscription, or coin packs) are processed by the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store, and managed on our behalf by RevenueCat. We never see or store your credit-card or payment details — those are handled entirely by the store. RevenueCat receives a purchase token and an anonymous app-user identifier so it can verify your purchase and let you restore it on a new device.

  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy · Google Play Privacy Policy

4. Optional cloud save

If you choose to sign in to Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android), your game progress can be backed up to your own account so it syncs across your devices. This is optional and controlled by you through those services. If you don't sign in, your progress simply stays on your device. This feature is governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

5. Children's privacy

Sayso is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app shows ads, we request non-personalised ads where a user is identified as a child in line with the stores' Families requirements. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will address it.

6. Data retention & security

Because your progress lives on your device, uninstalling the app removes it (unless you enabled cloud save, in which case it stays in your Apple/Google account until you delete it there). We don't operate a server that stores your personal data, so there's no central database of players to breach.

7. Your choices

  • Reset or opt out of your advertising identifier in your device settings.
  • Choose non-personalised ads when prompted (in regions where consent is required).
  • Remove ads entirely with an in-app purchase.
  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the app; manage cloud-saved data in your Game Center / Google Play Games account.

8. Third-party services

For transparency, the third-party services Sayso relies on are: Google AdMob (advertising), the Apple App Store / Google Play Billing with RevenueCat (purchases), and optionally Game Center / Google Play Games (cloud save). Each processes data under its own privacy policy, linked above.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves or as laws change. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be reflected in the app or on this page.

10. Contact us

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Email WeCraft Solutions at hassinewassef@gmail.com. We're happy to help.
