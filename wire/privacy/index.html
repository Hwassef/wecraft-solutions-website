WIRE — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

The short version. WIRE is a tech-news reader published by WeCraft Solutions. There is no account and no sign-up. We don't collect your personal information, we don't show ads, and we don't use analytics or tracking SDKs. Your bookmarks, settings and downloaded articles are stored on your device only.

This Privacy Policy explains how the WIRE app ("the app", "we", "us"), published by WeCraft Solutions, handles information when you use it. By using WIRE, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect, sell, or store personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, calendar, or location. We do not operate user accounts, and you never sign in to use the app.

We do not use advertising networks, analytics services, crash-reporting services, or third-party tracking SDKs of any kind. We do not use cookies or advertising identifiers.

2. Information stored on your device

Everything the app remembers about you stays on your phone, in the app's private storage:

  • Saved stories — the articles you bookmark, kept as a list of article references.
  • Your preferences — theme (light / dark / automatic), app language, article language, text size, and your alert and offline-reading toggles.
  • Recent searches — the last few search terms you typed, so you can tap them again.
  • Offline copies — when offline reading is enabled, the text of your saved stories is cached on the device so you can read without a connection.
  • First-run flags — simple markers that record you've already seen the setup screens.

None of this is transmitted to us. It is never uploaded, synced, or shared. Deleting the app removes all of it.

3. Network requests

To show you the news, the app requests articles, categories and search results from our content provider's read-only publishing API over an encrypted HTTPS connection. These requests contain only what is needed to return the right content — for example, the article or category you asked for and your chosen content language.

As with any internet request, the server that answers it necessarily receives your device's IP address and standard connection metadata in order to send the response back. We do not use this to build a profile of you, and we do not attach any identifier of you to these requests.

When an article contains images, your device loads those images directly from wherever the publisher hosts them, exactly as a web browser would.

4. Sharing an article

If you tap Share, the app hands the article's title and link to your device's own share sheet. What happens next is entirely up to you and to the app you pick (Messages, Mail, WhatsApp, etc.). We are not involved in, and do not record, that action.

5. Notifications

The "Breaking alerts" switch in Settings is a stored preference on your device. WIRE does not currently send push notifications and does not register your device for them. If we introduce push notifications in a future version, this policy will be updated before that feature ships.

6. No ads, no purchases

WIRE contains no advertising and no in-app purchases. There is no payment processing, and therefore no payment data of any kind.

7. Children's privacy

WIRE is a general-audience news reader and is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). Since we collect no personal information from anyone, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data retention & security

Because we don't collect your data, we hold nothing to retain. There is no user database to breach. Data created by your use of the app lives on your device, protected by your device's own security, and is removed when you uninstall the app. All network traffic between the app and the content API is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

9. Your choices

  • Change your theme, languages and text size at any time in You → Settings.
  • Turn offline reading on or off; turning it off stops articles being cached for offline use.
  • Clear your recent searches from the search screen.
  • Remove any bookmark at any time from the Saved tab.
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app.

10. Third-party services

WIRE relies on a single external service: the read-only publishing API that supplies the articles, plus the image hosts referenced inside those articles. There are no advertising, analytics, attribution, or social-login providers embedded in the app.

11. Your rights

Privacy laws such as the GDPR give you rights to access, correct, export or erase personal data held about you. Because WeCraft Solutions does not collect or store personal data from WIRE users, we hold no such data to provide or erase. You remain in full control of the data stored locally on your device, which you can delete at any time as described above. If you have a question about this, contact us and we'll answer it.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves or as laws change. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be reflected in the app or on this page.

13. Contact us

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Email WeCraft Solutions at hassinewassef@gmail.com. We're happy to help.
